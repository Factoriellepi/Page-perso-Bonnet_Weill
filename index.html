    Hello there! I am a postdoctoral fellow working at INRIA in collaboration with Mario Sigalotti of the team CAGE and Nastassia Pouradier-Duteil of the team MAMBA, both based at the Laboratoire Jacques-Louis Lions in Paris. 

    Between November 2019 and January 2021, I was a CNRS postdoctoral fellow at the Institut de Mathématiques de Jussieu under the supervision of Hélène Frankowska. From October 2016 to November 2019 , I did a PhD informally shared between the Laboratoire d'Informatique et Systèmes and the Dipartimento di Matematica ''Tullio
    Levi-Civita'' under the joint supervision of Francesco Rossi and Maxime Hauray. 

    My research interests primarily focus on multi-agent systems, optimal transport
    theory and Wasserstein calculus, control theory, and sub-Riemannian geometry.
    I also nurture some side interests in geometric measure theory, the calculus of
    variations and non-smooth analysis.
