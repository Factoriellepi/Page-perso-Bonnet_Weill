Homepage - Benoît Bonnet

Hi there, hello! I am a junior CNRS Researcher  working since January 2024 in the Laboratoire des Signaux et Systèmes (L2S) based at CentraleSupelec. My research interests focus primarily on multiagent systems and control theory, with a strong emphasis on optimal transport, set-valued and variational analysis as well as stability theory. I also developed broader taste for topics like the calculus of variations, measure theory, sub-Riemannian geometry, and more recently for stochastic calculus. 

Before joining the L2S, I spent a little more than two years in the MAC team of the Laboratoire d'Analyse et d'Architecture des Systèmes in Toulouse, also as a junior CNRS researcher. Prior to holding this position, I worked as a postdoc fellow at Sorbonne Université from October 2019 and November 2021, first under the supervision of Hélène Frankowska at the Institut de Mathématiques de Jussieu, and then with Mario Sigalotti and Nastassia Pouradier Duteil in the INRIA teams CAGE and MAMBA, both based at the Laboratoire Jacques-Louis Lions. I had previously done a PhD under the joint supervision of Francesco Rossi and Maxime Hauray from October 2016 to October 2019,  navigating between the Laboratoire d'Informatique et Systèmes in Marseille and the Dipartimento di Matematica ''Tullio Levi-Civita'' in Padova.
