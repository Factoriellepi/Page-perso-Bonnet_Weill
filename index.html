Hello there! I am a junior CNRS Researcher  working since November 2021 in the MAC team of the Laboratoire d'Analyse et d'Architecture des Systèmes in Toulouse. My research interests focus primarily on multi-agent systems, with a strong emphasis on control theory, optimal transport, set-valued and stability analysis. I also developed a broader interest for the calculus of variations, geometric measure theory, sub-Riemannian geometry, and more recently for stochastic calculus.   

From February to October 2021, I was a postdoctoral fellow working at INRIA with Mario Sigalotti and Nastassia Pouradier Duteil in the teams CAGE and MAMBA, both based at the Laboratoire Jacques-Louis Lions in Paris. Between November 2019 and January 2021, I was a postdoctoral fellow at the Institut de Mathématiques de Jussieu under the supervision of Hélène Frankowska. Prior to these postdoctoral positions, I did a PhD from October 2016 to October 2019 under the joint supervision of Francesco Rossi and Maxime Hauray, informally shared between the Laboratoire d'Informatique et Systèmes in Marseille and the Dipartimento di Matematica ''Tullio Levi-Civita'' in Padova, Italy.
